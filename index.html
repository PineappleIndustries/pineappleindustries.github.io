About Me

Hi! I am a fifteen-year old interested in coding and web development with very little knowledge in both fields.
I use He/Him pronouns, live with two cats (one of which is visible at the top of the page) and, as far as social media goes,
I am active on Discord with really no presence anywhere else except for Bandcamp and a few other places.

History

I as far as academic topics go, I quite enjoy history (though not over other subjects) and will generally get my information from podcasts in addition to school curriculum. I am particularly interested in the two world wars and have recently taken more interest in World War One, but find them both equally fascinating in different ways.

Music

I play and enjoy listening to music. I have been playing saxophone since the fifth grade and have been playing the tenor saxophone around four and a half years. I am learning the piano as well. I generally enjoy jazz/swing/blues music primarily, though derivations of those genres like electro swing and Herbie Hancock-style funk/jazz music are also in my interests. A few other genres and groups like Lofi music and Khruangbin (1960's Thai funk-inspired music) I also enjoy, but it mostly limited to the swing era and jazz.

Minecraft

Of course, as with most of my demographic, I play video games. The game I have been most interested for most of my life is Minecraft. I play Minecraft: Java Edition and take interest in the modding of Minecraft. If you are interested in a more detailed description of my Minecraft-related interests, feel free to check out the page featured on the navigation bar titled "Minecraft."
